 <blockquote>There is the Toymaker, the god of game. There is the Trickster, the god of traps. There is Maestro, the god of music. There is Reprobate, the god of spite. There is the Mara, the god of beasts. And the threefold deity of malice and mischief and misery. There are gods of skin and shame and secrets. There is Incensor ... the god of diaster, and her children called Doubt and Dread. And standing on high is the mother and father and other of them all. For the gods of all gods has returned. This is what I am called: I am called Glad-of-war, Grim, Raider and Third. I am one-eyed. I am also called Highest, and True-Guesser. I am Grimnir, and the Hooded One. I am All-Father, Gondlir Wand-bearer. I have as many names as there are winds. As many titles as there are ways to die. My ravens are Huginn and Muninn, Thoughts and Memory. My wolves are Freki and Geri. My horse is the gallowed. I am Odin!<blockquote>你认识我吗？你知道我是谁吗？你想知道我的名字吗？…… 以下都是我的名字：我被称为战神，葛林姆、奇袭者还有第三大神。我是独眼之神，也被称为最高主神、真理探询者。我是葛林姆尼尔，身披斗篷的神。我是全能之父，权杖之神。我有无数个名字，正如风有无数个称呼，死亡有无数种方式。我的神鸦叫胡因和穆因，即思想和记忆。我的宠狼叫弗雷奇和基利，我的爱驹是绞架。我就是奥丁！</blockquote></blockquote>

All these people who's ancestors devoted and sacrificed themselves to you. Can you hear them singing? Oh, you like to think you're a god, but you're not a god. You're just a parasite eating out the jealousy and envy and longing for the lives of others. You feed on them. On the memory of love and lost and birth and death and joy and sorrow. So, so come on then. Take mine. Take my memories. And I hope you've got a big appetite because I have lived a long life and I have seen a few things.<blockquote>你能听到吗？所有这些人一直生活在对你和你的审判的恐惧之中，所有这些人的祖先都奉献了自己，牺牲自己、奉献于你。你能听到他们在唱歌吗？哦，你把自己当作万物之神，但你可不是神，你只是一个被羡慕嫉妒恨侵蚀殆尽的寄生虫，觊觎着他人的生命。你以他们为食，以充斥着爱和失去、诞生和死亡、快乐和悲伤的记忆为食。所以……来吧……来带走我的吧，拿走那属于我的记忆。但我希望你胃口足够大，因为我活的很长而且所见不少。</blockquote><blockquote> I walked away from the last Great Time War. I marked the passing of the Time Lords. I saw the birth of the universe and watched as time ran out. Moment by moment until nothing remained -- no time, no space, just me. I walked in universes where the laws of physics where devised by the mind of a mad man. I watched universes freeze and creations burn. I have seen things you wouldn't believe. I have lost things you will never understand. And I know things. Secrets that must never be told. Knowledge that must never be spoken. Knowledge that will make parasite gods blaze. So come on then! Take it! Take it all, baby! Have it! You have it all!
</blockquote>我从最后的时间大战中抽身而去，我见证了时间领主的灭亡，我目睹了宇宙之诞生，也看惯了时间之流逝。一瞬又一瞬直至万物终结——没有时间，没有空间，只有“我”！我曾行走在那样的宇宙，其中的物理法则都出自于一个疯子的构想。我曾目睹宇宙冻结、造物焚毁，我曾目睹那些会令你无法置信的事物，我也曾失去过你永远无法理解的东西；我还知晓天机，知道那些永远不可泄露的秘密，知道那永远不可提及的知识，那些足以让神明灼烧的知识。所以，来吧！来拿吧！来拿走吧宝贝！拿走！全部都拿走！</blockquote>————————————————————
