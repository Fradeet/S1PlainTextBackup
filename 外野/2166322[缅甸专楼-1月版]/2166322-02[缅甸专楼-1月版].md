This development has raised concerns among investors and foreign governments, including those involved in the multi-billion dollar investment projects in Rakhine state.

若开邦有多支民族武装积极与缅军政府作战，若开邦是其中最大的一支。随着军力的增强和领土的扩大，若开军已占据了若开邦的大片领土，目前控制着该邦的几个城镇。这一事态发展引起了投资者和外国政府的担忧，包括那些在若开邦投资了数十亿美元项目的投资者和外国政府。

Among these investment projects, the deep sea port and Special Economic Zone in Kyaukphyu hold significant importance. The deep sea port, valued at an estimated USD 8 billion, is seen as a game-changer for Myanmar's trade and economic development. However, its security has become a pressing concern, particularly in light of the instability in Rakhine state.

在这些投资项目中，皎漂的深海港口和经济特区具有举足轻重的地位。该深水港估值80亿美元，被视为扭转缅甸贸易和经济发展的重要项目。然而，由于若开邦局势不稳定，皎漂港的安全已成为一个紧迫问题。

In response to the growing presence of the AA in the region, China has apparently shifted its focus towards negotiating with the AA. By engaging with the AA, China aims to safeguard its interests in Kyaukphyu, ensuring that its investments are protected and the project remains viable.

若开军在若开邦的控制范围日益增加，邻国显然已将重点转向与若开军谈判，通过与若开军接触，以维护其在皎漂的利益，确保其投资受到保护，项目持续可行。

On the other hand, China had previously attempted to directly deal with the junta regime in an attempt to maintain stability in Rakhine state. However, the junta regime's loss of control at the hands of the AA has led China to directly deal with the AA. With the AA vowing to liberate all remaining townships within its state, including Maungdaw, Sittwe, Taungup, Thandwe, Kyaukpyu, Gwa, Ann, and Manaung, China's ability to protect its projects is now in question.

另一方面，邻国此前曾试图直接与缅军政府交涉，以维护若开邦的稳定。然而，缅军政府对若开邦的控制落入若开军之手，这使得邻国不得不直接与若开军接触。若开军誓言要解放若开邦境内的所有城镇，包括孟都镇(Maungdaw)、实兑市(Sittwe)、洞鸽镇(Taungup)、丹兑镇(Thandwe)、皎漂镇(Kyaukpyu)、古瓦镇(Gwa)、安镇(Ann)和曼旺镇(Manaung)，因此邻国保护其项目的能力存在不确定性。

The AA's rising influence and the junta regime's weakening control have set the stage for negotiations between China and the Arakan Army. These negotiations aim to protect the interests of both parties involved, particularly in relation to the Kyaukphyu deep sea port and Special Economic Zone.

若开军影响力上升，缅军政府控制力减弱，这为邻国和若开军之间的谈判创造了条件。这些谈判旨在保护双方的利益，尤其是与皎漂深海港口和经济特区有关的利益。
